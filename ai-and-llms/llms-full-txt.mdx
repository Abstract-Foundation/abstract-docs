## When to use it

Use `llms-full.txt` when you want an AI tool to have **complete knowledge** of Abstract's documentation, not just an index. Good for:

- Uploading as a knowledge file to a [Claude Project](https://claude.ai)
- Full-context ingestion for a [ChatGPT](https://chatgpt.com) custom GPT
- Local RAG pipelines that need the complete documentation
